Vit Aaroh

We are the official Music/Instrument club of Vit Pune!
We have a lot of roles such as guitarist, pianist, drums, singers, violins, trumpet, etc.
We are always looking for new talent and would love for you to join US!

Music encompasses every aspect of life. Various enthusiasts, performers, students who thought of pursuing a career in music need a chance to present themselves, more so, a platform where someone can appreciate their talents. At Aaroh, we envision such a platform, along with a thriving community of music connoisseurs and performers alike. Provide a platform for budding musicians and enthusiasts by organizing events. To foster a lively and inclusive musical community within our college. We strive to offer a platform for talented musicians, singers and songwriters to showcase their abilities through performances, open mic nights, or jam sessions. We aim to cultivate a deep appreciation for music across all genres and styles among our members and the college community.
